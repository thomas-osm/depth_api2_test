Uploading Data

You may upload recored data to OpenSeaMap to help improve the bathymetric model and to receive good charts with rendered depths in return.
If you do not have a depth data account create one at the website.
You may then create a vessel configuration that holds the length draught and other parameters of your vessel on the webpage.
Switch back to the Wizard and enter your account credentials.


You may then choose a license and a vessel configuration. Note: Uploading is not possible without having set those fields.


Press finish and the upload process will commence.
